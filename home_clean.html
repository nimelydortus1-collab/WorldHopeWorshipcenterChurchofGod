About Us

Our church is dedicated to serving our members and the community with love and faith.

Our Mission

To spread the love of God, foster spiritual growth, and serve our community with compassion and dedication. We strive to create a welcoming environment where everyone can experience God's grace and find hope in His word.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and transformation in our community, building a church family that reflects God's love through worship, fellowship, and outreach. We envision a place where faith is strengthened, lives are changed, and God's kingdom is advanced.

Our Services

Sunday Worship

Join us every Sunday at 10:00 AM for an inspiring worship experience filled with prayer, music, and the Word of God.

Bible Study

Deepen your understanding of Scripture through our weekly Bible study sessions held on Wednesday evenings.

Youth Ministry

Our dynamic youth programs provide spiritual growth, fellowship, and community service opportunities for young people.

Weekly Activities

Participate in our diverse weekly activities including prayer meetings, outreach programs, and community engagement.

Yearly Activities

Experience our special yearly events and celebrations that bring the church community together in unity and joy.
